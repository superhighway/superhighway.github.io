5 Tips to Build Fast Websites

written on May 14, 2013 / performance, tools, web

Sometimes, you need to build websites for showcasing company information, team details, one main product information, and so on. Once you've done your beautiful sites, you wonder why your company page loads slowly. There is zero database calls, no back-end web app framework, no ops stuff... You scratch your head a couple of times; the pages are just freaking static sites that don't change much. And what's worse, everything is pretty much front-end stuff!

Take a deep breath.
