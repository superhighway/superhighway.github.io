Learning Design

Jan 30, 2013 / design, web

For the past two years, I have been dealing with implementing designs for iOS apps or web apps. I sometimes wonder how designers or front end developers can create cool-looking buttons, or create good-looking responsive websites.

I found these two small-but-concise books are really really great answers to my questions. I don't usually finish books, but for me, it took half a week to finish these.

  • Designing Web Applications by Nathan Barry
  • Responsive Web Design by Ethan Marcotte

There are other related books that I still plan to grasp, like A Book Apart Full Bundle and PeepCode's Design Bundle. Also, I have been trying to understand color theory.

I don't think I have 'good eyes', but I'll keep trying to find how much understanding I'll be happy with.
